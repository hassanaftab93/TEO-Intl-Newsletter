PLOT

TEO designed and developed a parking app for Plot, which aims to provide more and better parking spaces which in turn means less traffic, less pollution, and fewer frustrations. Our goal was to develop the app for both Android and iOS, and we wrote the back end in Microsoft .Net.
 
The app makes it easier for people to park their cars, and to report cars that are parked incorrectly
